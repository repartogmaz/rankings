Origin Story

From Zero
to Le Mans

Born in a small Colombian town with no racetracks, Sebastian dreamed of becoming a race car driver. From age 15, he worked multiple jobs to fund a passion the world didn't yet believe in.

His trajectory was set when a young Sebastian was trained for the first time by Juan Pablo Montoya himself, who immediately recognized the raw talent. Sebastian went on to claim go-karting championships and was named best rookie in his Formula Cars debut.

Then — at the peak of his momentum — a devastating off-track accident fractured everything. Multiple injuries. Career on hold. Back to zero.

Most would stop. Sebastian rebuilt. He sold car parts, restarted from scratch, re-entered motorsport as a coach for Ferrari and Porsche Championships — and used that platform to claw his way back into competition.

"Once in a decade talent who refused to let circumstances define his ceiling."

— The Racing Community
